About us

we specialise in delivering end to end IT consultancy services that drive clarity,

control and value across your asset, configuration and service management lifecycle. With deep expertise in ITSM strategy, CMDB governance and asset lifecycle optimisation, we partner with organisations to turn complexity into insight — and messy data into meaningful decisions.

With roots in operational excellence and hands on transformation, our founder has driven major initiatives across global enterprises and public sector organisations. our mission

Our mission at Crystal Horizon is to empower organizations with complete visibility over their IT assets, enabling proactive, informed decisions that drive efficiency, security, and growth.

We partner with you in:

any gaps, risks or inefficiencies in your asset/configuration practices.

data models, governance, tooling architecture, discovery integrations.

process embedment, dashboards, remediation programmes, continuous improvement.

what we do

  • Develop and execute ITSM strategy & roadmaps for Asset & Configuration Management.
  • Design and implement CMDB frameworks: data model, CI attributes, relationships and integrations with discovery tools (e.g., ServiceNow, BMC Remedy).
  • Standardise governance, policy, processes and tooling architecture to lift data accuracy, reduce duplicates/staleness and enable trusted insights.
  • Partner across business, technical and service management stakeholders (Change, Incident, Problem, Asset teams) to embed end to end configuration discipline.
  • Provide asset lifecycle and contract management consulting, with oversight of location, support coverage, renewals and service optimization.
